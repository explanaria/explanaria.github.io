Can you make a sphere using two different coordinates? Earth is a sphere, and many people use two coordinates named "latitude" and "longitude" to describe positions.

But at the poles, look carefully: the second, circular, coordinate stops being a circle and devolves into a point. An entire coordinate becomes completely useless! So we can't make a sphere using a coordinate system involving two independent shapes.
